<h1>General Exam Hints</h1>

<p>
This page contains general strategies and tips to help you prepare for the final exam.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Always justify your answers clearly.</li>
  <li>State definitions before using them in proofs.</li>
  <li>Check edge cases in counting problems.</li>
  <li>Use the Pigeonhole Principle carefully — identify “objects” and “boxes”.</li>
</ul>
