# Problem Paper 9 – Solutions


<section class="question-block">
  <div class="question-card">
    <h2>Question 1</h2>
    <p>Convert the following from degrees to radians.</p>
    <p>
      (a) \(80^\circ\) &emsp;
      (b) \(135^\circ\) &emsp;
      (c) \(200^\circ\)
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="solution-card">
    <h3>Solution</h3>
    <p>
      Recall that to convert from degrees to radians we use
      \[
      \text{radians} = \text{degrees} \times \frac{\pi}{180}.
      \]
    </p>

    <p><strong>(a)</strong> \(80^\circ\)</p>
    <p>
      \[
      80^\circ = 80 \times \frac{\pi}{180}
      = \frac{80\pi}{180}
      = \frac{4\pi}{9}
      \approx 1.39 \text{ rad}.
      \]
    </p>

    <p><strong>(b)</strong> \(135^\circ\)</p>
    <p>
      \[
      135^\circ = 135 \times \frac{\pi}{180}
      = \frac{135\pi}{180}
      = \frac{3\pi}{4}
      \approx 2.36 \text{ rad}.
      \]
    </p>

    <p><strong>(c)</strong> \(200^\circ\)</p>
    <p>
      \[
      200^\circ = 200 \times \frac{\pi}{180}
      = \frac{200\pi}{180}
      = \frac{10\pi}{9}
      \approx 3.49 \text{ rad}.
      \]
    </p>
    
  </div>
</section>
